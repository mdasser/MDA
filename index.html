About the Firm

Strong fundraising is built on strong operations.

MD Advisory is a specialized advisory firm focused on strengthening the operational infrastructure behind high-performing development and advancement organizations.

Many institutions invest heavily in fundraising talent but underinvest in the systems that support those efforts. We partner with universities, healthcare systems, and mission-driven institutions to build disciplined, scalable operational frameworks—so fundraisers can operate more effectively and deliver stronger outcomes.

Our approach is execution-focused and system-driven: we help organizations move from fragmented processes to integrated advancement operations where strategy, infrastructure, and performance are tightly aligned.
